About Us

Tradition since 2010

The Burger Joint was founded in 1998 by Mr. Smith in a small urban kitchen, where quality meets passion and every single ingredient is chosen with care. From our humble beginnings to our current city-wide reputation, we focus on the art of the grill and the joy of a perfect meal. Our team works tirelessly to source the finest local produce and premium meats to ensure that every customer leaves with a smile and a satisfied appetite. We believe in the power of a great burger to bring people together over honest, delicious food. We only use seasonal ingredients.
